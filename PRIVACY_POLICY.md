# Privacy Policy for Unfin

**Last updated:** February 2025

Unfin ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what data we collect and how we use it.

## Data We Collect

- **Account information:** When you sign up, we collect your email address, a password (stored securely), and an optional display name you choose.
- **Content you create:** Ideas you post, completions you add, and any text or attachments you upload are stored so we can provide the appâ€™s features (e.g. feed, explore, profile).
- **Usage data:** We may receive technical information (e.g. device type, app version) to operate and improve the service.

## How We Use Your Data

- To create and manage your account and authenticate you.
- To show your ideas and completions in the app (feed, explore, your profile) and to other users as intended by the app.
- To send you notifications you opt into (e.g. when someone completes your idea).
- To maintain security, prevent abuse, and improve the app.

## Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored using Supabase (hosted infrastructure). We use industry-standard security (including HTTPS and secure authentication) to protect your information.

## Sharing

- Your **display name** and **ideas/completions** you make public are visible to other Unfin users as part of the app.
- We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
- We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

## Your Rights

You can update or delete your account and content through the app (e.g. settings). If you want to delete your account or data, contact us using the support option in the app or the contact method listed in the App Store listing.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy here (or at the same URL) and update the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us via the support link in the app or on the App Store product page for Unfin.
